Information We Collect

We collect only the essential data required to synchronize your family life and ensure safety.

✓ Family Profile Intelligence Names, relationships, and biometric photos for authenticated profile access.
✓ Secure Health Vault Medical history, immunization logs, and prescriptions for the Medical Care tracker.
✓ Multimedia Assets Encrypted photos and videos posted to your private Family Memory Wall.
✓ Financial Metadata Categorized spending trends and budget limits within the Expense Tracker.

How We Use Your Data

Data processing is localized to your family unit to provide highly personalized automation.

✓ Milestone Tracking: Using development data to provide age-appropriate learning goals.
✓ Predictive Reminders: Notifying you of vaccine schedules and document expiries (Passports/IDs).
✓ Shopping Optimization: Generating curated newborn lists based on age-specific needs.

Sharing & Consent

Transparency is our foundation. We never monetize your family's personal information.

Professional Integration

Medical records are shared with "Doctor Connect" professionals only after you initiate a secure handshake and grant timed access.

Internal Permissions

The Family Admin manages granular controls, deciding which members can view specific health logs or financial reports.

Security Protocols

We deploy enterprise-grade shields to ensure your data remains inaccessible to unauthorized parties.

  • Zero-Knowledge Encryption: We cannot see your private documents; only you hold the keys.
  • Hardware Security: Support for FaceID, TouchID, and Physical Security Keys (MFA).
  • OCR Anonymization: Document scanning occurs via on-device processing where possible to limit cloud exposure.

AI Ethics & Logic

Our AI-powered features (Photo sorting, Health insights) are designed with "Privacy by Design."

BindFam AI does not use your family photos or medical data to train "Global Models." All learning is localized to your family group to ensure your private life never leaves your account's digital perimeter.

Children's Rights (COPPA/GDPR)

As an app focused on parenting, we treat child data with "Special Category" protection.

All data concerning minors is managed by a verified Guardian. We do not permit social sharing of child data outside the family hub without explicit, multi-step parental verification.

Data Retention

How long we keep your information depends on its purpose.

  • Active Records: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted Content: Purged from our active servers within 30 days of deletion.
  • Account Closure: Upon account termination, all family data is permanently wiped after a 60-day "Safety Grace Period."

Your Sovereignty

You have the right to Data Portability. Export your entire family legacy, including the Memory Wall and Health Records, into a standard format (JSON/PDF/CSV) at any time. We believe you should never be "locked in" to a platform.
